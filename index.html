How available are the web platform's features?

This lil' webapp indicates the percentage of users who have a browser that natively supports various web platform features. It's powered by data from caniuse.com and StatCounter, updated March 30, 2021, Obviously, this doesn't consider polyfill-ability or other fallback scenarios.
